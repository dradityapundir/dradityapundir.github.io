Dr. Aditya Kumar Singh Pundir

Professor | VLSI & Embedded Systems | Reconfigurable Hardware

About

Dr. Aditya Pundir has completed his Ph.D. in the area of VLSI & Embedded Systems / Reconfigurable Hardware with the research title “Fault Tolerant Reconfigurable Hardware Design using BIST Technique on SRAM”. He has more than 16 years of experience in industry as well as academia and is currently working as Professor at Arya College of Engineering & I.T.
