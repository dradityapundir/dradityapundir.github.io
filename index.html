Profile

Dr. Aditya Kumar Singh Pundir has completed his Ph.D. in the area of VLSI & Embedded Systems / Reconfigurable Hardware with the research title “Fault Tolerant Reconfigurable Hardware Design using BIST Technique on SRAM”. He completed his M.Tech. in VLSI Design from Rajasthan Technical University, Kota and B.E. in Electronics & Communication Engineering from Rajasthan University, Jaipur.

He is associated with the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering (NBA Accredited), Arya College of Engineering & I.T. (NAAC Accredited) as a Professor and has more than 16 years of experience in industry as well as academics.

Dr. Pundir has many Intellectual Property Rights (IPRs) to his credit, including five granted patents, six accepted design registrations, four registered copyrights, and the publication of 20 patents.

He has authored and edited several book chapters and six books, and has authored more than 50 research papers in peer-reviewed refereed journals including SCOPUS, SCI, and SCIE.

He has organized more than 30 workshops, summer internships, training programs, national seminars, and international conferences at various levels as Coordinator, Convener, Organizing Secretary, and Chair. He has also worked as a Session Chair, Conference Steering Committee Member, Editorial Board Member, and Reviewer in international and national journals and conferences.

He has published six edited books titled “Recent Trends in Communication & Intelligent Systems” by Springer. An edited book titled “Advances in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning” was published by IOP Science.

He is also a Guest Editor for several journal special issues, including:

  • International Journal of Cognitive Informatics and Natural Intelligence (IJCINI), IGI Global
  • International Journal of Computational Complexity and Intelligent Algorithms, Inderscience

Research Interests:

  • AI in Memory Testing & Repair
  • Built-in Self-Test using Embedded System Design
  • Machine Learning
  • Algorithms for CAD
  • Signal Processing

Dr. Pundir is a Life Member of the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) and the Computer Society of India (CSI). He is also a Professional Member of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) and IEEE.
