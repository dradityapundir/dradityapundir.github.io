About

Dr. Aditya Pundir has completed his Ph.D. in VLSI & Embedded Systems / Reconfigurable Hardware with specialization in fault tolerant reconfigurable hardware design using BIST techniques on SRAM. He has more than 16 years of experience in industry and academia and is currently working as Professor at Arya College of Engineering & I.T.
