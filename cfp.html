Submission Guidelines

Author Guidelines

  • All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference.
  • Manuscripts must be submitted in PDF format through Microsoft CMT.
  • Author name and affiliation must not appear in the manuscript (double-blind review).
  • Minimum paper length: 10 pages.
  • Similarity score must not exceed 10%.
  • Papers exceeding similarity limit shall not be processed.

Review Process & Oral Presentation

  • Papers with similarity score less than 10% will be processed for double-blind review.
  • All submissions will be peer-reviewed by the Technical Committee.
  • Paper status will be informed as:
    • (a) Accepted for Oral Presentation
    • (b) Accepted with Minor Corrections for Oral Presentation
    • (c) Rejected

Paper Submission Link

Authors must create a free account on Microsoft CMT and choose the role as Author. After login, select “Enter as an Author” for paper submission.

Submission Portal:
