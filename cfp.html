Submission Guidelines

All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference. Manuscripts must be submitted through the Microsoft CMT system in PDF format without author names and affiliations, as per the prescribed format (see Downloads).

  • Minimum paper length: 10 pages
  • Similarity score must be ≤ 10%
  • Papers exceeding similarity limits will not be processed

Review Process & Oral Presentation

  • Papers with similarity below 10% will undergo double-blind peer review.
  • Reviews will be conducted by the Technical Program Committee.
  • Decisions will be:
    1. Accepted for Oral Presentation
    2. Accepted with Minor Corrections
    3. Rejected
