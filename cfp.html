Submission Guidelines

  • Manuscripts must be original and not submitted elsewhere.
  • Submission through Microsoft CMT in PDF format (double-blind).
  • Minimum paper length: 10 pages.
  • Similarity index must be ≤ 10%.

Review Process & Oral Presentation

  • Double-blind peer review by the Technical Committee.
  • Decision categories:
    1. Accepted for Oral Presentation
    2. Accepted with Minor Corrections
    3. Rejected
